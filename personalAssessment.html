Self Development - ePortfolio

Critical Thinking and Problem Solving

Problem-solving is most predominant in my software development. Whether it is an assignment or personal project, I always try and pick the correct tool and use it effectively as possible to achieve the required result. For Example, I used C++ for an application that was heavy on data manipulation for the speed and React for a website that emphasises responsiveness and interaction.

Critical Thinking is visible in most things I do, whether it is in my assignments or worth ethic. I always try and improve what I have previously made, even if it has received positive feedback. I do this to improve myself and any projects and stay ahead of the competition.

Professionalism

I have worked for my college in the past, creating a parent portal from scratch and making it fit the college's style. This was my first professional job as a web developer, and it taught me how to create a website for a strict specification. It also taught me how to act within a development team and the workflow involved.

I have had two jobs in the catering industry which have both required interactions with the public. In both positions, I needed to maintain a professional demeanour to represent the company accurately. In the future, I would like more experience working in a professional environment to get me used to the standards involved.

Communication Skills

I have been employed in the catering industry twice, Costa and McDonald's, both of which has required me to communicate effectively and professionally to customers and colleagues. Without this communication, I would not be able to function on a given station and fall behind.

I have also been employed as a software developer, which required me to convey my intentions/decisions for a task to the rest of the team to make sure it meets the required specification. I did this effectively enough for all my code to be used in the full deployment of the website.

Teamwork and Leadership

I have been involved with multiple hackathons and often lead my team, not always to victory but to a finished, usable product. I have had 2 jobs (McDonald's and Costa), both of which can be quite intense at times. Both positions require me to work as a team to provide excellent service to customers and keep a calm environment.

I sometimes do lack the confidence to speak up about a matter regarding my commitment. For example, a project that would require me to dedicate time to it. I will try and speak up for myself in the future.

Learning and Research Skills

In the sense of researching in the hopes of finding something or an understanding that is entirely new is alien to me. However, researching a topic and finding any useful resources or techniques is something I have done quite a lot of in the past; particularly when starting a new project. I would often try to complete a task in an unnecessarily complicated fashion, in the hopes of picking up a new skill.

For example, using C++ & Electron (Node.JS) for my coursework when most used VB. Or using React + Redux to build a website instead of something like Wordpress which would have been easier.

To improve I will try and gather more information on a process before attempting to use it within an app, to evaluate whether it is useful in the first place.
